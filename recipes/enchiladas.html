Angela's Awesome Enchiladas

The secret to these awesome chicken enchiladas is in the seasonings I whipped these up on my own using ingreddients I had on hand. Shredding the chicken is the most time-consumign step, but its's worth it the end. Serve with sour cream and a side of spanish rice. Give thsese babies a try!

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breat meat- cut into chunks
  • 1 1/4 cups sour cream
  • 1 can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 bunch of green onions, chopped, divided
  • 1 can chopped green chiles, drained
  • 1 package mild taco seasoning mix
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powderx
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 5 flour tortillas
  • 3 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 can enchilada sauce
  • 1 can sliced black olives, drained

Steps:

  1. Step 1: place chicken into a large pot and add water to cover. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until chicken is no longer pink and the juices run clear, about 10 minutes.
  2. Step 2: remove from the pot and let sit until cool enough to handle, 5 to 10 minutes. Shred chicken with tow forks
  3. Step 3: combine sour cream, condensed soup, and chili powder in a saucepan. Bring to a simmer, stirring occasionally, then turn off the heat and cover to keep warm
  4. Step 4: at the same time, melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion; cook and stir until translucent, about 5 minutes.   5. Step 5: preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  6. Step 6: fill each tortilla with 1/5 of the chicken mixture and about 5 tablespoons of cheddar cheese

  7. Step 9: Bake in the preheated oven untill filling is heated through and hceese is melted and bubbbling, about 25 minutes
